Family Photography

These captures the relationship between parents,children,siblings and the extended family.
Any family occasions is a golden chances to practice the art.
Family photos not only show connection and tender moments caught between family members, but expresses authentic feeling.

Portrait Photography

Portraits tend to aim towards capturing a group or a persons personality.
Apart from lighting and backdrops,poses contributes greatly to it's making.

Street photography

Street photography is capturing scene in public places.
It is administered for art or research that features empirical chance encounters and random
occurrences.
Subject matter doesn't always need to include people.
It tends to be less deliberate in it's defined messaging.

Wildlife Photography

Of all kinds of photography,the more time one spends with their subject, the more likely ones'
images will be intimate and revealing and for this kind of photography one needs to:

  • Be Patient
  • Have a Good gear
  • Be at a Safe shooting location

Candid Photography

This is an unplanned moment.
It removes the posed aspect of the equation,
shots taken of people in motion,spontaneously or by surprise.
Candid photography aims to capture subject in natural state without needing to direct or pose them.

Golden-hour Photography

This kind of photography is taken an hour before sunset or an hour after sunrise which
the sun is at it's lowest point in the sky.
With this it produces a warm golden tone as background lighting.
